文法測驗

  1. The new policy, _ was announced yesterday, will take effect next month.
    A. which
    B. that
    C. what
    D. where
  2. Employees are required to submit the report _ Friday at the latest.
    A. until
    B. by
    C. during
    D. since
  3. Not only _ the deadline, but he also failed to inform his manager.
    A. he missed
    B. did he miss
    C. he has missed
    D. has he missed
  4. The conference was postponed _ the flight delay caused by the storm.
    A. because
    B. because of
    C. despite
    D. although
  5. Ms. Chen is responsible _ negotiating contracts with overseas partners.
    A. to
    B. for
    C. with
    D. on
  6. The more carefully the data is analyzed, the _ the final results will be.
    A. accurate
    B. more accurate
    C. most accurate
    D. accuracy
  7. The proposal must be approved _ it can be implemented.
    A. until
    B. unless
    C. before
    D. whereas
  8. He denied _ the confidential information to the press.
    A. to release
    B. release
    C. releasing
    D. released
  9. All candidates _ complete the application form by the end of this week.
    A. would
    B. may
    C. must
    D. might
  10. The manager asked whether the shipment _ on schedule.
    A. arrives
    B. has arrived
    C. will arrive
    D. had arrived
  11. _ the high cost, the company decided to proceed with the project.
    A. Because
    B. Although
    C. Despite
    D. Due
  12. The equipment should be inspected regularly to ensure that it _ properly.
    A. operate
    B. operates
    C. is operated
    D. has operated
  13. Mr. Brown, along with his assistants, _ attending the annual meeting.
    A. are
    B. were
    C. is
    D. have been
  14. The contract specifies that payment _ within 30 days of delivery.
    A. is made
    B. be made
    C. was made
    D. has made
  15. Had the technician followed the instructions, the system _ malfunctioned.
    A. would not have
    B. will not have
    C. did not
    D. has not
